The Problem

Crypto was never built for people who can't type.

Every wallet, every DEX, every bridge assumes a user with two working hands, clear vision, and the motor control to approve a pop-up in under 60 seconds.

For the 1.3 billion people living with disabilities, and the pioneers already using Neuralink, that assumption is a wall.

Our Solution

Thoughts First. Keys Never Leave You.

  • ◆Voice today: Advanced speech recognition and natural language AI parse your words into verified on-chain intents.
  • ◆BCI tomorrow: A Brain Signal Mode ready for Neuralink and next-gen EEG inputs.
  • ◆Self-custody always: WalletConnect v2 in a Telegram Mini App, we never touch your keys.
How It Works

Four steps. Zero keyboards.

  1. 1

    Think or Speak

    "Send 20 USDC to Alice on Solana." A thought, a whisper, that's it.

  2. 2

    AI Parses Intent

    Our AI engine decodes amount, token, recipient, and chain with human-level precision.

  3. 3

    Preview & Sign

    Gas, route, destination, all verified on-screen, then signed in your own wallet via WalletConnect.

  4. 4

    Confirmed on-chain

    Hash, explorer link, receipt, delivered back to you in Telegram in seconds.

Features

Built for everyone
, optimized for the 1.3 billion.

Brain Signal Mode

Neuralink-ready channel that accepts EEG/BCI intents, verified through a multi-step confirmation loop to prevent false positives.

Voice Fallback

Advanced speech recognition transcribes your voice, and our AI engine interprets intent. Speak naturally, no command syntax to memorize, no button mazes to navigate.

WalletConnect v2

Signing happens in a Telegram Mini App via Reown AppKit + ethers.js. MetaMask, Trust, Rainbow, Phantom, no private keys ever touch our servers.

Multi-Chain Native

BSC, Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, Solana, Tron, and Bitcoin, all from a single conversational interface.
